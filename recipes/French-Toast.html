You will experience a culinary combination like non other from your past. You will be let down by all other breakfasts you have from this point going forward. INGREDIENTS

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • salt to taste
  • 6 THICK slices bread
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

Lets make some French Toast, :)

  1. Gather all of your ingredients in one spot so that you dont forget anything like i do.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt all together in a shallow bowl so that it gets all mixy mixy
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skilly and heat over medium-high heat. Not too hot now, dont wanna burn or cook the eggs you silly goosey.
  4. Dunk that nice soft supple sensual loaf in the egg mixture, soaking both sides nicely with all that juicy egg
  5. Put that soaked bread on the skillet, which should be nice and hot now. Cook until golden, 3-4 minutes.
  6. Serve that ish and let everyone know how good of a cook you are.
