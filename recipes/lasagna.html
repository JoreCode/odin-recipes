THE Best Lasagna you are ever going to eat in your life

So im going to explain to you how to make the best lasagna you ever had in your life on god, no cap, frfr. This shit will be so bussin that you will probably nut, and im seriously not even kidding. The mixture of ingredients, combined with the finnesse and percision that you will execute to create this will be so mind blowing and unbelievable that you will not be able to contain yourself. INGREDIENTS

  • Sweet Italian Sawsage
  • Lean ground BEEF
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomato
  • 2 Cans Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Cans Tomato Paste (paste deez nuts)
  • 2 Tablespoons of white sugar
  • Parsley
  • Dried Basil
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles (duh)
  • Cheese (Parmesan, MUZZ, Rigot)
  • egg

Let's cook this hoe

  1. Cook sausage, beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until browned. Stur in crushed Tomato, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar to make it sweet like your sister, 2 tbsp parsely, Basil, 1 tsp salt, italian seasoning, fennel, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for like 1.5 hours, stir every now and then like a good boy.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, make it salty like you after I hang out with your mom. Cook lasagna Noodles in the water for like 8 minutes. Drain those noods, rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine the rigot with egg, the last of the parsely, and 1/2 tsp salt.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375F (190C if you are a commie)
  4. Now lets build this. Spread 1.5 cups of my meat in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noods lengthwise over my meat. Spread (like your mom) with half the rigot. Top with 1/3 of muzz slices. Spoon (like i do with your sister) 1.5 of my meat over the muzz, and sprinkle 1/4 cup Parm. Repeat the layers and finish it off with teh muzz and parm. Cover with foil. If you dont want it to stick (which you shouldnt cuz you arent literally stupid) spray the foil with cooking spray.
  5. Last step. Pop that sucker in the oven for 25 solid minutes. Remove the foil and bake for ANOTHER 25 minutes. Let it rest for 15 after that. Youll wanna dive in, but if you do everything will fall apart like your parents marriage. Be patient. The nut is worth it.
